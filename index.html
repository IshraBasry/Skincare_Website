About the Event

The Jardin de Mer Skincare Experience is an event focused on exploring the natural ingredients from the sea and plants to enhance your skincare routine. Designed for skincare enthusiasts, beauty lovers, and wellness seekers, this event will guide you through simple, effective tips and techniques. Join us to learn how to use ocean and botanical-based products to nourish and improve your skin's health in a simple and approachable way.
